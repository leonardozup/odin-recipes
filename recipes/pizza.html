Pizza Margherita is the timeless Italian classic featuring a perfect harmony of fresh tomato sauce, creamy mozzarella cheese, and aromatic basil on a crispy yet tender crust. This simple yet elegant combination celebrates the pure flavors of quality ingredients, making it the beloved foundation of authentic Neapolitan pizza tradition.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 500g bread flour or tipo 00 flour
  • 325ml warm water
  • 7g active dry yeast
  • 10g salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Toppings:

  • 200g crushed San Marzano tomatoes (or quality tomato sauce)
  • 250g fresh mozzarella cheese (preferably buffalo mozzarella)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil for drizzling
  • Sea salt to taste
  • Black pepper (optional)

Optional flavor enhancers:

  • 1-2 cloves garlic (minced into sauce)
  • Pinch of sugar (for sauce balance)
  • Grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese

Steps to Make Pizza Margherita

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water and let it foam for 5 minutes
  2. Mix flour and salt in a large bowl, then add yeast mixture and olive oil
  3. Knead the dough for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic
  4. Place dough in oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1-2 hours until doubled
  5. Preheat oven to highest temperature (usually 500°F/260°C) with pizza stone if available
  6. Punch down dough and divide into 2-3 portions for individual pizzas
  7. Roll or stretch each portion into thin circles on floured surface
  8. Transfer dough to pizza pan or parchment paper
  9. Spread thin layer of tomato sauce, leaving 1-inch border for crust
  10. Tear mozzarella into pieces and distribute evenly over sauce
  11. Drizzle lightly with olive oil and sprinkle with salt
  12. Bake for 10-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly
  13. Remove from oven and immediately top with fresh basil leaves
  14. Let cool for 2-3 minutes, then slice and serve hot
